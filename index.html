TrackHub FAQS

We're here to answer all your questions.

Whether you’re new to TrackHub or already on board, we’ve got the answers you need. Explore our FAQs to learn how we make shipping seamless for your business.

What is TrackHub?

TrackHub is a logistics platform that partners with top carriers to handle shipping for e-commerce businesses, offering real-time tracking and reliable delivery solutions.

How does TrackHub handle shipping?

Once you provide your shipping requirements, we coordinate with our trusted carriers to pick up, deliver, and track parcels securely and efficiently.

Can I track my shipments in real-time?

Yes! Our platform provides live updates, so you and your customers can track orders at every stage of the shipping process.

Which carriers does TrackHub work with?

We collaborate with leading carriers such as DHL, UPS, Chronopost, and many others to ensure fast and reliable shipping.

How can I start shipping with TrackHub?

Contact our team, share your requirements, and we’ll create a customized plan to get you started quickly.

What if my customers need to return an item?

TrackHub offers return management services to simplify the process for both you and your customers.
